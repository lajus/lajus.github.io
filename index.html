About me

I am a newly qualified high school teacher in Computer Science. I also continue to maintain the AMIE project.

And before,

I was a PhD student in the DIG Team at Telecom ParisTech (Paris, France). I worked on rule mining in incomplete knowledge graphs under the supervision of Fabian Suchanek.

Master degree in Computer Science ("D&K") obtained in 2016. Former student of ENS Cachan (now known as ENS Paris-Saclay).
